My Top 3 Pics of The Best Series of 2023

These are the best series by far according to Mwakamuyi. Mwakamuyi is the world leading film ranking specialist, who has spent the last 15 years analysing and finding the best films. Today, Mwakamuyi takes us through his 2023 top pics for the best series.

Money Heist

Money heist is the most popular Spanish Netflix show, it portlays how a genius named the professor robs the Spanish central bank with his intelingence.

The most amazing and mesmerising thing about this series is the level of planing that the professor puts into his roberies, he calculates every possible probability of what may go wrong and how get out of such a situation.

Prison Break

Micheal commits a crime deliberately to go to prison with the aim of getting his brother who was wrongfully accused of murdering the vice president's brother. He does everything humanlly possible to ensure that he breaks his brother out of prison.

One of my favourite lines from the series was by Michael, when he said:
“Preparation can only take you so far. After that, you have to take a few leaps of faith.”

Breaking Bad

This mesmerising series showcases how a chemistry teacher struggling to pay bills and has only a few months to live uses his chemistry knowledge to become the biggest drug lord.
